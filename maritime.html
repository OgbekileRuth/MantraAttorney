Maritime & Aviation Law



Our firm maintains an active and growing practice in the areas of International Trade, Maritime, and Transportation Law, offering comprehensive legal services to both domestic and international clients involved in cross-border commerce, shipping, logistics, and related sectors.

Members of our team possess deep expertise in transportation and admiralty matters, enabling us to provide tailored legal solutions across a wide range of transactions and disputes. Our experience spans charter-party disputes, cargo claims and defences, carriage contracts, and sale and purchase disputes, all of which are approached with a sound understanding of both commercial imperatives and legal complexities.
We also routinely advise on the structuring and negotiation of packaging and financial arrangements, including documentation for supply and syndication transactions, as well as vessel acquisition, vessel registration, and regulatory compliance. Our services extend to facilitating high-value infrastructure and logistics projects, such as aviation terminal development and tanker construction transactions, in collaboration with engineering and regulatory stakeholders.

In all matters, we are committed to securing practical and commercially viable outcomes for our clients, while maintaining full compliance with the laws and regulations governing international trade and transportation. Our approach is proactive, industry-informed, and designed to navigate the intricate web of domestic and international regulatory requirements—including those of maritime and aviation authorities, trade finance institutions, and cross-border commerce regulators.

We take pride in our ability to blend legal precision with commercial insight, ensuring that our clients’ interests are protected and advanced in every transaction or dispute under our care.
