Corporate & Commercial Regulatory



Our firm brings to bear a wealth of expertise and practical experience in handling corporate affairs and regulatory compliance for entities with both local and foreign participation. We are unwavering in our commitment to delivering highly professional, responsive, and tailored legal services to meet the specific needs of our diverse clientele.
We routinely advise on and facilitate complex and non-complex corporate matters, including but not limited to:

The firm renders legal service on all aspects of operations in this field of law including but not limited to the following:

  1. Incorporation of new businesses and non-profit organizations, in full compliance with applicable regulatory frameworks;
  2. Procurement of requisite licenses, permits, and approvals, including Business Permits and Expatriate Quota allocations for foreign-participating companies;
  3. Completion of all relevant post-incorporation filings with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) and other statutory bodies;
  4. Corporate governance advisory and due diligence investigations, ensuring compliance, risk mitigation, and business sustainability.
  5. Corporate search.

Our approach ensures that all services are rendered with the highest standards of confidentiality, diligence, and attention to detail.
Specifically, our firm provides comprehensive legal services in the following areas:

  1. Choice, structure, and registration of corporate entities – We advise clients on the most suitable legal structure for their operations, taking into account tax, liability, ownership, and regulatory considerations.
  2. Drafting and review of simple and complex legal documentation – Including shareholders’ agreements, joint venture contracts, service agreements, and other bespoke commercial instruments tailored to the client’s business objectives.
  3. Post-incorporation services, company restructuring, and winding up – We support clients through changes in company structure, board reconstitution, increase in share capital, mergers and acquisitions, and lawful winding-up procedures.
  4. Enterprise and business name registration, and post-registration services – Ensuring timely and accurate filings and compliance with statutory obligations.
  5. Corporate search and verification services – Including legal status checks, beneficial ownership identification, and corporate compliance evaluations for M&A and investment purposes.
